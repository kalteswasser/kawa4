Use Case

Make Eye-Catching Popups—Quickly and Easily

Autodealers love Sleeknote’s user-friendly popup builder. With our drag-and-drop editor and 120+ conversion-ready templates, you can create brand-centric website popups in minutes—both for desktop and mobile.

 

Offer Test Drives

Get more test drive bookings, on auto-pilot, without a rep’s assistance. Use our Page Level feature to target prospects browsing a certain model or retarget returning visitors.

Contact Warm Leads

Offer sales-ready prospects a free valuation for their car. Target new or returning visitors on certain pages such as new arrivals, dealer reviews, and more. Add or remove the input fields you need and you’re good to go.

The Features You Need to Grow Your Dealership

Advanced Scheduling

Managing multiple time-sensitive campaigns is a challenge for any marketer. Use our Advanced Scheduling feature to enable or disable campaigns automatically.

Countdown Timer

We found from our research of 1+ billion popups that campaigns with countdown timers boost conversions by 112.93 percent. Add countdown timers to drive more interest in daily deals, last-minute bookings, and more.

Geo-Targeting

Target visitors based on where they’re located, and personalize your campaign(s) message to promote local dealerships. You can even localize your popup copy based on location to drive further engagement.
