Use Case

Make Eye-Catching Popups—Quickly and Easily

Enterprise marketers love Sleeknote’s user-friendly popup builder. With our drag-and-drop editor and 120+ conversion-ready templates, you can create branded website popups in minutes—both for desktop and mobile.

 

Turn Website Visitors into Leads

Invite first-time website visitors, through a campaign, to join your email list. Or, use our Cookie Detection feature to exclude returning subscribers and create a better user experience.

Use our Multistep feature to learn more about a lead’s company size, budget, and more—without losing conversions.

Make Brand-Centric Popups

Easily make popups that match the look and feel of your site. Use our signature drag-and-drop builder to create eye-catching popups. Upload custom fonts and images to keep your popups brand-centric.

Sell More Products / Services

Offering an in-store experience is different online. Give visitors the help they need and welcome return visitors by reminding them their favorite items are back in stock. Integrate with your favorite product recommendation engine to target shoppers with the right offer at the right time.

The Features You Need to Grow Your Enterprise

Multistep

Go beyond asking website visitors for a name and an email address. Use our signature Multistep feature to get more details from new subscribers, such as gender and preferences, without losing conversions.

Geo-Targeting

Target visitors based on where they’re located, and personalize your campaign(s) message to invite shoppers to pick up their order in-store.

You can even personalize your popup copy based on location to drive further engagement.

SiteData Merge Tags

Use SitaData Merge Tags to personalize a campaign’s copy based on any visitor data. Get more leads by adding a visitor’s industry to the campaign’s headline. Welcome back a returning subscriber by mentioning their name in the campaign’s copy.
