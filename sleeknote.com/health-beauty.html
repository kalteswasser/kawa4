The Features You Need to Grow Your Health & Beauty Store

Multistep

Go beyond asking website visitors for a name and an email address. Use our Multistep feature to get more details from new subscribers, such as gender and preferences, without losing conversions.

SiteData

Show a campaign based on any visitor data. Invite buyers to spend more to qualify for free shipping. Recommend a similar item based on what’s in a shopper’s cart. Replace copy based on a shopper’s details. The possibilities are endless.

Geo-Targeting

Use our Geo-Targeting feature to target visitors based on where they’re located, and personalize your campaign(s) message to promote last-minute deals. You can even localize your popup copy based on location to drive further engagement.
