Is This for You?

Does this sound familiar?

Our Three-Step Process for Success

It’s important that you know how we work and what the process looks like. So, here’s our three-step process for increasing conversion rates on your site.

1. Planning

First, we schedule a 30-minute meeting where we define your conversion goals and decide on the best strategy for your business.

2. Execution

Based on your conversion goals, we design tailored user tests, report our results, and finally, propose solutions to increase conversion rates.

3. Evaluation

At the end of the project, we will evaluate the results we’ve achieved and discuss potential next steps.

What’s in It for You?

Higher Conversions

It sounds simple, and it is. When you know why visitors don’t convert, you can target those specific areas and increase conversion rates.

Put Visitors in Focus

Instead of optimizing based on gut-feeling, you’ll get a plan for optimization based on data from the people who actually use your website.

Thorough Testing of Your Site

We’ll conduct at least 5 different user tests on your site to identify conversion gaps and problem areas that need optimizing.

Ready-to-implement Design

We’ll create a new design for the problem areas on your site that you can just send to your web developer for implementation.

User Tests

We’ll design user tests specifically for your site, and observe test subjects perform various tasks on your site. We’ll do five user tests on desktop and five user tests on mobile. This will provide valuable inputs for how you can increase conversion rates.

Expert Examination

We always go through your site and make recommendations based on our experience and previous user tests. We’ll identify low-hanging fruit on both desktop and mobile that can easily be changed for higher conversions.

Presentation Meeting

We’ll present our findings and ideas from the user tests and our own examination on a video call. Then we’ll settle on what needs to be changed specifically on the site to increase conversion rates.

Custom Redesign

We’ll create a redesign of your site based on the findings from the presentation meeting.

Delivery Meeting

We’ll present the new design on a video call. The design will be illustrated in detail so it’s easy for your web developer to implement it.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get started?

The intro meeting lasts 30 minutes. This is where we get to know your visions and goals and create a plan for how we achieve (and surpass) your goals.

Will you execute in line with our brand?

No need to worry about us understanding your brand or your way of communicating. The most important thing to us is that we do it your way—and we’ll do everything we can to understand your wishes during the intro meeting.

Is there a lot of back-and-forth communication?

We try to keep communication to the necessary minimum. However, you’ll always be able to get in touch with your dedicated Success Manager if you have questions. No standard contact forms; just a direct line to your Success Manager who is more than ready to speak with you.
